Calico

Calico is a coat color, not a breed of cat.

Felis Catus is the scientific name for the domestic cat.

  • Scientific Name: Felis Catus
  • Average Length: 76.2 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 10-15 years
  • Habitat: Indoor

More than 99% of calico cats are female.
Male calico cats are extremely rare.
Symbols of luck, strong-willed & temperamental, but also very sweet & affectionate. Cannot be bred, their coloring is a genetic accident. Known to be spirit guardians.
